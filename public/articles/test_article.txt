* Introduction
This is a sample article written in org-mode. Org-mode is a powerful system for organizing your notes, tasks, and projects.

* Main Content
** Section 1: What is Org-Mode?
Org-mode is an Emacs mode for keeping notes, maintaining TODO lists, planning projects, and authoring documents with a fast and effective plain-text system.

** Section 2: Features of Org-Mode
- Hierarchical organization of data
- TODO lists with progress tracking
- Scheduling and deadlines
- Exporting to various formats (HTML, PDF, etc.)

* Conclusion
Org-mode is a versatile tool that can help you stay organized and productive. Give it a try and see how it can improve your workflow!
